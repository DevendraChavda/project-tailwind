Website SEO audits

Everything from load time and mobile friendliness to crawlability and indexing is subject to analysis in a website SEO audit. With the help of this in-depth analysis, companies may better optimize their websites for search engines by fixing any problems that come to light. With the appropriate strategy, a technical SEO audit can increase a company's ranking in organic search results and bring in more targeted visitors.

An audit of your website's SEO should take into account the following main elements:

  • Your website structure
  • Your website analytics
  • Your Google Search Console data
  • And more

Since lots of technical aspects are involved. Only a skilled person should put their hands into the fire. Broken Links, Internal Links, On-Page SEO, Indexability, Redirects, XML Sitemaps, Organic Rankings, Google Search Console, Google Analytics, Page Speed, Core Web Vitals, and many other little things are counted here.

Keyword research

It's easy to skip the keyword research step when developing an SEO plan for a business's website. But you can't undervalue it; you should be aware of these search terms that people are using to locate your company online since you own it.

But if you don't do keyword research first, you may waste time and money by ending up bringing the wrong user onboard. A wrong keyword can give the search engine a wrong summary of your website.

It helps your company reach potential clients or customers by adapting to how they search. In yourAlso, there are fair chances that you're setting yourself up for failure with some poorly chosen and executed keywords. Two of the major areas of concern are

  • Low Search Volume Keyword
  • Incorrect usage of the keywords

In order to get the information your customer is looking for from Google, you might conduct keyword analysis to find out what they are searching for or what term they are using in their searches.

For example, if you own a personal training company, keyword research will happen for phrases like "best chest workouts for men," "best exercises to lose fat," "best personal training company near me," and so on.

Content Creation and Implementation

Consistently producing SEO-friendly material is a struggle, but it pays off pretty well by increasing your site's organic search exposure, rankings, and visitors.

Content and SEO, when polished together, can propel a site to the top of Google's search results. But that's just when they're really performing well.

  • The term "content" refers to the information or any material on your website that’s intended to entertain its visitors.
  • The term "SEO" refers to how the information or material of a piece of content is being updated and showcased on the website listed on the search engine.

Here are some of the most popular content categories and how your site's visitors can benefit from them.

  • Product pages: are essential to the success of any online store. An effective product page acts as a landing page to transform the coming search traffic into prospective customers
  • Blog pages: One of the simplest methods to produce high-quality SEO content on a regular basis is via a blog. Blog entries are an excellent strategy to increase your site's authority.
  • Articles: Consider a headline story, interview, or news report. This is the bread and butter of most online platforms, which will attract traffic from articles.
  • Lists:The content is more digestible in listing format. Titles have a higher click-through rate in both search engine results and social media feeds. It can draw results, especially when the listing is properly formatted.
  • Guides: Guides are typically presented on websites that sell products that require special instructions to guide their customers. Guides have been found to boost website traffic by multiple folds because they attract organic searches by answering people's queries
  • Infographics:With plenty of data (usually in the form of graphs or charts) about a single topic, Infographics tend to get a lot of attention and links. Nevertheless, the rest of the website needs careful optimization because so much of the content is incorporated inside. Still, the image, caption, and other visual matrices are also observed by search engines.
  • Slideshows:A slideshow could be a serial killer for website performance. If they are not working well, it will lead to lag and delays in the website's browsing experience.

How to choose the best SEO management company?

When it comes to organic search traffic, rankings, and improved conversion, knowing how to hire an SEO firm can make all the difference. Some of our customers came to us after a bad experience caused their website to drop in rankings.

As a certified SEO service provider, we don’t want you to make the wrong decision. We want you to make the right choice instead by following the standard criteria.

  1. Review the portfolio of an agency.

    See their work on their website. Is this company's site SEO-friendly and professional? If they have built a great internet presence, they will probably help you.

  2. Know your requirements.

    It is advisable for every client to first gain a basic understanding of SEO before seeking any clarification. Having the basic knowledge might help you understand the value of the service that the company is committing to you.

  3. No one can guarantee results.

    Avoid companies that "guarantee" Google rankings or site traffic. Google regularly refines its search algorithm. And it is also our best practice to keep sharing regular updates with you in this regard.

  4. Check Reviews

    Online testimonials could have been the best way to understand a company's legitimacy. It would be a cherry on top if the company had proof of working with other clients.

  5. Expect Transparency

    Complete SEO information from a firm is a good indicator. Avoid companies offering false claims. SEO isn't magic. A competent SEO company will explain their approach like we do.

  6. Cheap, fast, and easy don’t exist.

    SEO involves effort and money, even though internet marketing is the cheapest. To ensure the SEO company maintains results and promotes your site without pause, a monthly or annual contract is best.
