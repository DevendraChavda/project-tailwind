O U R

Goal Statement

The webdew family believes in action, progress, and growth. The young startup has achieved several milestones in its active years till now, but nothing keeps us from seeking more and more. Since more and more people seem to indulge in video, we aim to break our own record in hours of video produced. That's not all, we are also determined to become HubSpot Elite Partners by the end of 2022.

OUR

Journey

Come join us in our journey to growth and betterment.

FOUNDED

webdew was co-founded by Mr. Danish Wadhwa and Mrs. Chehak Wadhwa, to quickly become the top marketing agency in India, and soon one of the most recognized in the world.

2016

BECAME A HUBSPOT PARTNER

After a year of rapid growth, webdew became a certified HubSpot Partner, a leading marketing automation platform, as we funneled down more leads throughout the year.

2017

BECAME HUBSPOT GOLD PARTNER

With hardwork and commitment, we acquired the status of HubSpot Gold Partners by the end of 2018. The growth didn’t stop us there, and went on to introduce Video Production as our services.

2018

YEAR OF GROWTH

2019 was the year of automation. We introduced new tools such as Time Doctor, Flock, and Click-up to automate operations and boost our productivity. It was commendable how our team adapted to the changes.

2019

BECAME HUBSPOT DIAMOND PARTNER

Despite hardships, we moved from HubSpot Gold Partners to HubSpot Diamond partners in 2020. We complied with the new normal and transitioned to a work-from-home structure, and kept up our productivity.

2020

ACHIEVEMENT YEAR 2021

Achieving the targets in this uncertain year was not easy. But we made considerable progress to be better than we were in 2020. In 2021, we completely revamped our website, breaked our own records of creating and producing videos and content, both in numbers & quality, and generated a good amount of revenue.

2021

OUR GOAL FOR 2022

We always look ahead and strive to achieve more. For 2022, our goal is to become elite partners, get more retained clients, build a strong team of 80+ and generate double the revenue than 2021.

2022

MORE GOALS FOR 2023

Our goal for 2023 is to establish ourselves as a leading player in the SaaS video service industry. Despite not meeting our targets in previous years, we are more determined than ever to become Elite partners this year and become a significant player in the Marketplace.

2023
